Uzor Somtochukwu

"The Young Owner" | Civil Engineer & Entrepreneur

Born: February 2, 2006

From: Imo State, Nigeria

Uzor Somtochukwu, popularly known as "The Young Owner," is a Nigerian Civil Engineer, entrepreneur, and founder of Fashionbing. At just 18 years old, he became the youngest founder of a major fashion firm in Nigeria. Known as a civil engineering prodigy and visionary, Uzor has inspired many with his journey from a humble background to building impactful ventures.

With a passion for both structure and style, Uzor blends engineering with creativity, pushing the boundaries of what's possible for young minds in business and innovation.
